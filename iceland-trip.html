🚗 Tesla Tips

😴
Camp Mode
Keeps cabin at 21°C during Ida's naps without running the engine. Tesla menu → Safety → Camp Mode. Uses minimal battery.
🚨
Klaustur is Non-Negotiable
May 17 is 500+ km. The Kirkjubæjarklaustur Supercharger on the return is the only charger between Jökulsárlón and Vík. Do not skip it.
🗺️
Use Tesla Navigation
Enter destination the night before. Tesla auto-routes via Superchargers and calculates exactly when to stop. Trust it.
❄️
Cold Reduces Range
May in Iceland: 5–12°C. Cold weather reduces range 10–20%. Don't let battery drop below 15% in rural areas.
🛣️
Road Tax 2026
New Iceland law: 8.35 ISK/km (6.95 gov + 1.40 Hertz fee) on actual km driven. Added to your bill at return.
🚫
No F-Roads
Tesla cannot go on F-roads (highland tracks marked F on maps). All your planned stops are on paved Ring Road — you're fine.

👧 Toddler Tips for Ida

🍫
Ris Bars
Keep a stash in the car at all times. Essential for toddler bribes at transitions, car seat protests, and long drives.
🌊
Reynisfjara Safety
Hold Ida at ALL times. Stay 20m+ from the water. Sneaker waves strike without warning and are fatal. No exceptions.
🚶
Carrier vs Stroller
Many viewpoints have gravel or uneven paths. A baby carrier gives more flexibility than a stroller at scenic spots.
🌡️
Layering for Ida
Iceland May: 5–12°C with wind chill. Pack thermal base layers, waterproof jacket and trousers, mittens, and hat.
😴
Nap Strategy
Schedule long drives during Ida's nap window. Camp Mode keeps her warm. May 17 morning drive (Vík → Jökulsárlón) is perfect nap time.
🏊
Geothermal Pools
Ida can go in most pools. Water is ~38°C — warm and shallow areas available. Mandatory shower before entry (bring her shampoo).

🌍 Iceland Practical Tips

💧
Free Tap Water
Iceland's tap water is world-class and free everywhere. Never buy bottled water. Fill bottles at every stop and hotel.
🛒
Bónus Supermarket
Iceland's cheapest supermarket chain (yellow pig logo). Stock up Day 1 & 2 to save a fortune on expensive restaurant meals.
🌤️
Weather Changes Fast
Check vedur.is every morning. Pack waterproof layers even on sunny days — conditions can flip in 20 minutes.
🌌
Northern Lights
Late May is marginal but possible. Check vedur.is aurora forecasts. Drive 20 min from any town for dark skies if forecast is good.
💳
Cards Everywhere
Iceland is virtually cashless. Cards accepted absolutely everywhere including tiny roadside stalls. No need for ISK cash.
🦺
Rain Covers
Bring rain covers for the stroller and waterproof layers for everyone. Gullfoss and Skógafoss will soak you — this is fun, not a problem.
