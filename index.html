🚗 Tesla Tips

😴
Camp Mode
Keeps cabin at 21°C during Ida's naps at stops. Tesla menu → Safety → Camp Mode. Uses minimal battery.
🚨
Klaustur is Non-Negotiable
May 17 is 500+ km. Kirkjubæjarklaustur is the ONLY charger between Jökulsárlón and Vík. Do not skip it under any circumstances.
🗺️
Use Tesla Navigation
Enter destination the night before. Tesla auto-routes via Superchargers and tells you exactly when to stop. Trust it.
❄️
Cold Reduces Range
Iceland May: 5–12°C. Cold reduces range 10–20%. Don't let battery drop below 15% in rural areas.
🛣️
Road Tax 2026
New law: 8.35 ISK/km on actual distance. ~CAD $90 for your trip total. Added to Hertz bill at return.
🚫
No F-Roads
Tesla cannot go on F-roads (highland tracks). All your stops are on paved Ring Road — you're fine.

👧 Toddler Tips for Ida

🍫
Ris Bars
Keep a stash in the car at all times. Essential for toddler bribes at transitions, car seat protests, and long drives.
🌊
Reynisfjara Safety
Hold Ida at ALL times. Stay 20m+ from the water. Sneaker waves strike without warning and are fatal. No exceptions.
🚶
Carrier vs Stroller
Many viewpoints have gravel or uneven paths. A baby carrier gives more flexibility. Use carrier at Fagradalsfjall volcano.
🌡️
Layering for Ida
Iceland May: 5–12°C with wind chill. Thermal base layers, waterproof jacket and trousers, mittens, hat — essential.
😴
Nap Strategy
Schedule long drives during Ida's nap window. Camp Mode keeps her warm. May 17 morning drive is perfect nap time.
🐦
Arctic Terns at Grótta
Arctic terns nest in May and will dive-bomb anyone near their nests — exciting not dangerous. Ida will be absolutely delighted.

🌍 Iceland Practical Tips

💧
Free Tap Water
Iceland's tap water is world-class and free everywhere. Never buy bottled water. Fill bottles at every stop and guesthouse.
🛒
Bónus Supermarket
Iceland's cheapest supermarket (yellow pig logo). Stock up Day 1 & 2 to save a fortune on expensive restaurant meals.
🌤️
Weather Changes Fast
Check vedur.is every morning. Pack waterproof layers even on sunny days — conditions can flip in 20 minutes.
🌋
Volcano Safety
Check vedur.is and safe.is on May 19 morning before visiting Fagradalsfjall. Area may be closed if eruption is active.
💳
Cards Everywhere
Iceland is virtually cashless. Cards accepted everywhere including tiny roadside stalls. No ISK cash needed.
🧴
Pool Etiquette
Shower before entering any pool — strictly enforced. Bring Ida's shampoo. No swimwear in the shower area.
